Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?
You can enroll in any course by browsing our course catalog, selecting your desired course, and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through the registration and payment process.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and popular payment gateways in Sri Lanka. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Can I access courses on mobile devices?
Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You can learn anytime, anywhere.
Do you provide certificates after course completion?
Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our courses. These certificates are recognized by industry professionals and can be shared on LinkedIn.
How can I contact my instructor?
Once enrolled in a course, you'll have access to our learning platform where you can message your instructor directly, participate in discussion forums, and join live Q&A sessions.
